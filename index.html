Important Guidelines for Parents

  • Homework check: See if the kids have completed their homework.
  • Educational content: Check if there are copies, pencils, dotpens, etc.
  • Cleaning out school uniform: Ensure the school uniform is neat and fits well.
  • Personal cleaning: Focus on cleaning nails, hair, hands, and feet.
  • Homework together: Get homework done by sitting with the children after school.
  • Activities all day: Ask about school activities.
  • Peaceful Environment: Avoid fighting at home in front of children as home is their first school.
  • Use of decent language: Family members should not use bad words in front of children.
  • Teaching love and respect: Teach the younger ones to love and respect the elders.
  • Felicitation to guests: Teach children to respect guests who come home.
  • Self-reliance: Teach children to wash their own clothes and dishes.
  • Agricultural work: Engage in farming work during holidays.
  • Conversation with School: Understand children's progress by visiting school or contacting the principal or class teacher weekly.

These things play an important role in the educational and personal development of children. Thank you.
